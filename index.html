WHO WE ARE

Schrodinger Technologie Ltd. is a Nigerian-based social enterprise committed to solving one of humanity’s most urgent challenges ensuring universal access to clean water while fostering environmental sustainability and climate resilience. Established with a clear mission to transform water access in underserved and climate-vulnerable communities, we work at the intersection of renewable energy, water technology, and social impact to deliver innovative, sustainable, and affordable solutions.
